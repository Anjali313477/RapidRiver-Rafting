River Rafting~

What to know

Uttarakhand is one of the premier destinations for river rafting in India, offering an exciting blend of adrenaline, adventure, and natural beauty. The state is home to some of the most famous rivers, including the Ganges, which flows through stunning landscapes, offering thrilling rapids for beginners as well as experienced rafters. Popular rafting locations like Rishikesh, Kaudiyala, and Shivpuri provide the perfect backdrop for rafting adventures, surrounded by hills, forests, and the spiritual aura of the region. Rafting in Uttarakhand is a must-do for adventure lovers. The rapids range from grade I to grade IV, offering something for everyoneâ€”from gentle stretches for beginners to thrilling, high-intensity rapids for seasoned rafters. Whether you want to experience a peaceful ride through the holy Ganges or tackle more challenging rapids, Uttarakhand has it all. Besides the thrill of rafting, the area offers a chance to witness the natural beauty of the Himalayas and pristine riverscapes.

Best Time to Visit

The best time for river rafting in Uttarakhand is from September to June. The weather is favorable during these months, offering clear skies and perfect water levels for rafting. Avoid the monsoon season (July to August), as the rivers tend to swell, making rafting dangerous.

Camping in Rishikesh

Camping in Rishikesh is a unique blend of adventure, tranquility, and natural beauty. Nestled along the banks of the sacred Ganges River, surrounded by lush forests and the majestic Himalayan foothills, Rishikesh offers an ideal environment for outdoor enthusiasts. Campers can indulge in thrilling activities such as river rafting, trekking, and bungee jumping, while also enjoying peaceful moments of meditation and yoga by the river. The campsites offer basic to luxurious accommodations, providing a cozy stay under the stars. The cool evening breeze, the sound of the river, and the scenic landscapes make camping in Rishikesh a rejuvenating and unforgettable experience for nature lovers and adventurers alike.
